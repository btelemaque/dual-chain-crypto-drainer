DUAL CHAIN DRAINER — Threat Research Report
March 30, 2026
An independent investigation into an active cross-chain cryptocurrency wallet drainer campaign targeting Ledger, Trezor, MetaMask, and Phantom wallet holders simultaneously across Ethereum and Solana. The campaign delivers worthless dust tokens to over 161,000 victims to lure them into fake DeFi claim sites, where a ClickFix social engineering technique silently injects a malicious PowerShell payload into the victim's clipboard.
Full analysis covers the complete five-stage attack chain, malware reverse engineering of the recovered C2 implant and 117,000-line JavaScript drainer kit, and a 9-hop on-chain financial attribution trail connecting current campaign infrastructure to verified accounts on US-regulated exchange Bittrex and Russian exchange EXMO — opened in 2016 and connected to BTC-e, the cybercrime exchange seized by the US DOJ in 2017. Identified proceeds: $620,000+ across 33 blockchain networks. Campaign reported to FBI IC3 (submission ID: c0880f13e56d4432a89aea4b0f56425e).
